Ryan Thomas Gosling (born November 12, 1980) is a Canadian actor. (1995) and Goosebumps (1996). His breakthrough film role was as a Jewish neo-Nazi in The Believer (2001), and he gained stardom for starring in the 2004 romantic drama The Notebook.
